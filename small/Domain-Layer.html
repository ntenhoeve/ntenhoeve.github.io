Domain Layer

The d omain Layer also known as business layer, business logic layer, domain model layer.
It is responsible for representing concepts of the application domain, information about the domain situation (state), and domain rules. State that reflects the domain situation is controlled and used here, even though the technical details of storing it are delegated to the infrastructure layer. This layer is the heart of your application.
