Infrastructure Layer

The infrastructure Layer is also known as data access layer , persistence layer , logging, networking, and other services which are required to support the service or domain layer.
It provides generic technical capabilities that support the higher layers such as:
  • Sending and retrieving a message for the application,
  • Store and retrieve domain objects in a database (persistence)
  • Drawing widgets for the UI,
  • etc.
